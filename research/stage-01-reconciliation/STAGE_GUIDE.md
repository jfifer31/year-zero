# Stage 1R – Human Baseline Reconciliation

## Purpose

Reconcile the completed education-facing Stage 1A and general-human Stage 1B into one reviewed, two-layer human baseline. This is the only Stage 1 component permitted to authorise Stage 2.

## Authorised inputs

- Stage 1A canonical files in `research/stage-01-human-baseline/`;
- Stage 1B canonical files in `research/stage-01b-general-human-baseline/`.

No new sources may be collected during reconciliation. If a gap requires new evidence, return it to the relevant component stage.

## Classification framework

Every candidate must be classified as:

- general human baseline;
- adolescent or education-context modifier;
- convergent across both components;
- component-specific;
- contradictory or unresolved;
- excluded from downstream use.

Apparent convergence must be checked for shared sources, repeated constructs and non-independent syntheses.

## Required output structure

The reconciled report and pass-forward must preserve:

1. general human drives and social structures;
2. adolescent and educational-expression modifiers;
3. disagreements, uncertainties and limits;
4. explicit separation between empirical findings and later normative reasoning.

The reconciliation may not derive education's purpose, define flourishing, design capabilities or begin curriculum work.

## Gate rule

Stage 2 remains blocked until Stage 1R receives `pass` or `pass_with_cautions`. Only `research/stage-01-reconciliation/PASS_FORWARD.md` may then authorise Stage 2.
