## Claims Not to Overextend

| Claim | Permitted interpretation | Prohibited overextension |
|---|---|---|
| Foundation models are improving. | Model-plus-system capability is progressing in some domains. | Scaling guarantees AGI or reliable autonomy. |
| Inference-time compute helps. | Search/test-time compute can improve some tasks at cost. | More inference compute guarantees real-world performance. |
| Coding agents are improving. | They may accelerate bounded, testable software work. | They replace software engineers or own open-ended projects. |
| AI can assist AI R&D. | AI may contribute to bounded R&D workflows. | Recursive self-improvement is established. |
| GUI agents can operate interfaces. | Some workflows may become agent-mediated. | Safe state-changing autonomy is solved. |
| Robotics is progressing. | Structured and semi-structured deployment may grow. | Robotics follows software timelines or demos prove general labour replacement. |
| AI-for-science is useful. | It can accelerate selected search and candidate-generation tasks. | AI guarantees cures, validated materials or solved engineering. |
| Governance frameworks exist. | They structure obligations and risk management. | They prove safety or enforcement capacity. |
| Synthetic media is risky. | It raises verification costs and context-specific trust vulnerabilities. | It automatically collapses truth or democracy. |
| Open/edge AI spreads capability. | It can broaden access and local control. | It is simply democratic, safe, dangerous or uncontrollable. |

## Required Cautions for Stage 4

- Use technical condition bands, not a single AI future.
- Keep future-facing claims labelled as projections or assumptions.
- Do not collapse technical capability into social adoption.
- Do not use benchmarks or demos as deployment proof.
- Preserve infrastructure and access inequalities.
- Preserve the difference between digital feedback, physical embodiment, scientific validation and social trust.
- Do not derive education’s purpose, curriculum, capabilities, subjects, pedagogy or assessment from Stage 3 alone.

---

## Revision Triggers

Reopen Stage 3 if:

- independent long-task evaluations show reliable day/week-scale autonomous performance;
- production coding-agent evidence shows low defects and manageable review burden at scale;
- verified AI-created improvements materially accelerate AI R&D;
- robotics deployments show low-intervention, low-cost, safe operation across varied environments;
- AI-designed drugs/materials/engineering systems achieve validated deployment milestones;
- compute, energy, chip or data-centre constraints materially change deployment trajectories;
- open/edge/sovereign AI materially alters governance or access assumptions;
- major cyber/bio/synthetic-media incidents provide stronger operational evidence;
- companion or ambient-AI evidence materially changes relational risk assessment;
- new evidence corrects the high-resource/frontier-lab bias of the current source base.
