# Stage 2 Pass-Forward — Human Flourishing Model

> **Authority status:** `authorised_pass_with_cautions`
> **Stage review:** `pass_with_cautions` (22 June 2026).

## Controlled thesis

Flourishing is the developing and revisable capacity to live a life one has reason to value: with sufficient bodily and material security, credible agency, growing competence, relationships that permit recognition without domination, sources of meaning that can be questioned, and opportunities for responsible contribution that do not depend on coerced sacrifice.

**Classification:** integrated Normative Judgement informed by bounded empirical findings. It carries no empirical confidence label and is not a validated scale or discovered architecture of human nature. [Ledger: `S2-NRM-007`, `S2-TRC-001`]

## Normative judgements authorised as project commitments

1. **Equal standing:** worth is not conditional on achievement, conformity, happiness or usefulness. [`S2-NRM-008`]
2. **Floor and freedom:** protect a limited floor of bodily integrity, safety, material security, equal standing and real opportunity while preserving plural good lives. [`S2-NRM-001`]
3. **Competence without supremacy:** value expanding effective and responsible action, not productivity, credential or rank as the master good. [`S2-NRM-002`]
4. **Recognition without domination:** value safe support and reciprocal care alongside privacy, difference, dissent and credible exit. [`S2-NRM-003`]
5. **Contribution without coerced sacrifice:** value age-appropriate participation, care and repair only with fair burdens and voice; usefulness is not the price of worth. [`S2-NRM-004`]
6. **Evolving agency:** respect present voice and developing capacity through proportionate, explainable and reviewable protection. [`S2-NRM-005`]
7. **Anti-reduction:** no indicator or composite may become a complete judgement of a person. [`S2-NRM-006`]

These are value choices. Later stages must not cite them as empirical human facts.

## Working assumption carried visibly

The project treats resources, rights, safety, power and institutions as conditions shaping real opportunities and reported well-being. The reviewed sources are primarily conceptual, official and cross-national frameworks rather than a direct causal review; this premise must not be represented as one settled causal or political account. [`S2-CLM-006`]

## Authorised flourishing dimensions

- bodily and material security;
- credible agency;
- developing competence;
- recognition and care;
- meaning and orientation;
- responsible contribution;
- plural identity and future possibility.

The list is a provisional decision framework. Dimensions overlap, may conflict and have no authorised universal weights.

## Binding trade-off rules

- Take first-person appraisal seriously without allowing satisfaction alone to erase deprivation, danger or domination.
- Permit endorsed interdependence and obligation while auditing power, burden and credible refusal.
- Permit worthwhile challenge while rejecting avoidable harm and resilience romanticisation.
- Protect belonging and continuity while preserving privacy, difference, critique and exit.
- Treat purpose as psychologically consequential but morally contestable.
- Balance present adolescent welfare and voice with future options; neither automatically overrides the other.
- Stage 2 authorises no individual flourishing instrument, score, threshold or diagnostic use. Any future proposal requires a separately authorised measurement purpose, construct-validity and invariance evidence, fairness and harms review, privacy safeguards and a new gate; it inherits no authority from this model.

## Prohibited downstream uses

Do not:

- create a composite flourishing score or rank learners by this model;
- infer an individual's motive, maturity, mental health, worth or potential from any dimension;
- treat autonomy as independence, competence as achievement, belonging as conformity, resilience as endurance, meaning as moral truth or contribution as compulsory service;
- universalise Western measures, family forms or individualist accounts of agency;
- derive curriculum purposes, capabilities, subjects, pedagogy or assessment directly from this memo;
- substitute flourishing language for safeguarding, material provision, rights or mental-health care.

## Uncertainties carried forward

Cultural transportability; endorsement under dependency; invisible and gendered care burdens; disability, impairment and distributed capability; present/future trade-offs; moral evaluation of purposes; protection of solitude and low-social-intensity lives; and measurement without optimisation remain open. [`S2-UNC-001`–`S2-UNC-008`]

## Revision triggers

Reopen Stage 2 if downstream work turns the dimensions into a total person score, hides a culturally narrow ideal, treats productivity as flourishing, demands belonging or service, romanticises adversity, erases disability/support needs, or uses the common floor paternalistically without voice and review.
