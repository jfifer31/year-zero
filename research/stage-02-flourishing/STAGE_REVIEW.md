## Bias and generalisability disposition

The evidence remains English-language, culturally uneven, frequently Western, school-enrolled, self-reported and observational. Adult evidence is used only with local age cautions. Disability, neurotype, language, gender, poverty, institutional access, absent/disconnected learners, chosen solitude and non-standard relational profiles remain explicit uncertainties. No recurrence is universalised and no group result may diagnose an individual.

## Adversarial disposition

The model explicitly resists happiness reduction, productivity capture, coerced belonging, sacrificial contribution, resilience romanticisation, paternalism and composite-score optimisation. These safeguards are conditions of any eventual pass, not decorative caveats.

## Final gate

The focused re-audit returned **PASS** after all blocking corrections. The stage decision is `pass_with_cautions`.

Conditions: empirical associations remain population-bounded; the integrated model remains normative; the structural premise remains a Working Assumption; no individual flourishing instrument or score is authorised; cultural, disability, power, coercion and adolescent-scope cautions travel with downstream use; and no curriculum derivation is authorised here.
