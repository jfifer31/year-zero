## 2. Universal release boundaries

Version 0.1 must not be presented as proof that:

- the curriculum improves learner outcomes;
- its capability or evidence models are complete or valid for high-stakes use;
- it works across all learners and contexts;
- its future conditions are predictions;
- it should replace formal education or recognised qualifications.

Stage 11 must revise and extend this list using the actual release evidence.
