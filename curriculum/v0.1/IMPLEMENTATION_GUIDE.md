## 13. Safeguarding and privacy

Implementation must follow `docs/ETHICS_AND_PUBLIC_TESTING.md`. Do not collect or publish learner data, work, images, videos or AI logs through public contribution routes.

## 14. Feedback to the project

Use broad, anonymised observations through the implementation-feedback issue template. Do not treat implementation feedback as proof of impact unless it was gathered through an appropriate study.

## 15. Implementation checklist

- [ ] The use is authorised by the released framework.
- [ ] Experimental status is clear.
- [ ] Legal, statutory and safeguarding duties remain in force.
- [ ] Evidence collection is proportionate and private by default.
- [ ] Accessibility and low-resource alternatives have been considered.
- [ ] AI use, if any, has a defined learning purpose and non-AI alternative.
- [ ] Stage 11 has reviewed the guidance.
