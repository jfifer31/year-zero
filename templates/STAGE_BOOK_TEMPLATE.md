- repository references;
- file-path language;
- agent/process meta-text;
- report-style scaffolding;
- generic chapter summaries standing in for scenes, arguments or examples;
- source dumps;
- unsupported certainty;
- later-stage curriculum, capability, pedagogy, subject or assessment decisions.

Major claims should cite valid source IDs or ledger IDs lightly and readably.
